Guidelines

  • 1.Reservation follows a FIRST COME, FIRST SERVE BASIS.
  • 2.To give ample time for the admin to setup your reservation. It is advised to make your reservations one week ahead of your desired schedule.
  • 3.An email notification will be sent out to the user's registered email address for approved reservations.
  • 4.Cancellation requests can be done any day before the scheduled reservation. The admin will assess the reason for cancellation and decide whether to accept or deny the request.
  • 5.Requisitioners are advised to come to their respective schedules one hour before their schedule.

About us

FaRes is made by two college students namely Genten Lalas and Ramon Delos Reyes. They envision to make reservations in DLSU-D to be easy and accessible online.

FaRes is an online reservation website that is specially made for DLSU-D. It has it's own online payment system and inside it are the facilities available for students, professors or guests to reserve.

Our team wishes to innovate reservations made inside DLSU-D. The reservation process is quick and simple. We provide a 360 view of the facilities without needing you to go on the spot
